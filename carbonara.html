Carbonara

>

Ingredients:

  • Egg
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Guanciale
  • Parmigiano Reggiano
  • Spaghetti

Steps:

  1. Cut the Guanciale into thick shapes
  2. Finely grate the parmiginia
  3. Whisk the egg, cheese, and pepper together
  4. Cook the pasta
  5. Reserve the pasta cooking water
  6. Cook guinaciale until golden while the pasta is cooking
  7. Toss pasta in guinaciale
  8. Transfer into sauce bowl
  9. Add 1/2 cup of the pasta cooking water
  10. Mix vigorously
  11. Serve
