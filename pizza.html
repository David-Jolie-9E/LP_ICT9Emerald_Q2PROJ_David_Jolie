Pizza

>

Ingredients:

    For dough:

  • Warm Water
  • Dry Yeast
  • Bread Flour
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Kosher Salt
  • Sugar

    • For topics:

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozerella Cheese
  • Mushrooms
  • Basil Leaves

Steps:

  1. Proof the yeast
  2. Make and knead the pizza dough
  3. Let the dough rise
  4. Preheat the pizza dough
  5. Divide the dough into two balls
  6. Prep the toppings
  7. Flatten the dough ball and stetch out in a round circle
  8. Brush the dough top with olive oil
  9. Spread tomato sauce and fill with toppings
  10. Slide in oven and bake
  11. Serve
