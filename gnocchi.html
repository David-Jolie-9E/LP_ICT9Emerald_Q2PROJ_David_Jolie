Gnocchi

>

Ingredients:

  • Olive Oil and Butter
  • Garlic
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Tomato Paste
  • Chicken Broth
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Gnocchi
  • Cheese
  • Basil

Steps:

  1. Add the oil and butter on a skillet on medium heat
  2. Once hot, at the garlic and and cook for 30 seconds
  3. Add gnocchi and stir slowly for 5-7 mins so it doesn't stick to the pan
  4. Give it a taste
  5. Once cooked, add the mozzarella, parmesian, and basil leaves
  6. Serve immediately
