Spaghetti Marinara

>

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 gloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 teaspoon, dried oregano
  • Kosher Salt
  • 1 28-ounce can whole peeled tomatoes, crushed by hand
  • 1/2 cup chopped basil
  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into cubes

Steps:

  1. Combine olive oil and garlic in a large skillet on medium heat until golden brown
  2. Add onion, oregano and 1 teaspoon of salt
  3. Cook until the onion is soft but not browned for 10 mins.
  4. Add tomatoes and their juices and 1/2 cup of water and continue cooking until the sauce is slight reduced for 20 minuutes
  5. Stir in basil and reason with salt and keep it under low heat
  6. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil
  7. Add pasta and and cook as labels direct. Save 1 cup of cooking water then drain
  8. Add cooking paste to the sauce along with 1/2 of reserved cooking water
  9. Increase the heat to medium and toss to coat, adding the remaining cooking water as needed to loosen the sauce. Transfer the pasta to bowls and drizzle with olive oil.
